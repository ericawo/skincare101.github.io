moisturizer

Using a moisturizer/ moisturizing product is crucial for a skincare routine! A good moisturizer will help prevent dry skin and can even prevent wrinkles.

Here are some ingredients to look out for:

  • Hyaluronic acid: a humectant (aka draws moisture into the skin) allowing your skin to be hydrated without feeling heavy or greasy.
  • Ceramides: can restore your skin's natural barrier and overall health by retaining moisture and averting harmful elements.
  • Vitamin C: an antioxidant useful for preventing UV damage, and producing collagen, and overall brightening the skin.
