sunscreen

Sunscreen is an important part of the skincare routine because it can prevent aging and protect your skin against harmful UV rays that increase the chance of skin cancer. It also helps prevents scarring and discoloration from worsening. Many recommend purchasing sunscreen with SPF 30+ for better defense against the sun.

*The SPF number tells you the how much longer it would take to get a sunburn when using the product versus the amount of time without any sunscreen. EX: SPF 50 means it would take 50 times as long to get sunburned if you weren't wearing any sunscreen.

Here are some ingredients to look out for:

  • Mineral Sunscreens: relies on ingredients to work as a shield to block the sun's rays
  • Chemical Sunscreens: relies on ingredients to absorb the sun's harmful rays
