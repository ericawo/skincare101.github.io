cleanser

Cleansing is often done twice a day, but those with dry or oily skin may prefer to cleanse only in the evening to remove any sweat, dead cells, debris, makeup, etc. that are accumulated in the morning.

Common ingredients:

  • Chemical Exfoliants: AHAs (such as glycolic acid and lactic acid) and BHAs (such as salicyclic acid) are essential for cleaning pores. While AHAs exfoliate by cleaning the surface of the skin, BHAs exfoliate by cleaning inside the pore and on the surface of the skin.
  • Vitamin C: an antioxidant useful for preventing UV damage, and producing collagen, and overall brightening the skin.
  • Benzoyl Peroxide: an antibacterial ingredient that cleans the inside of the pore and the surface of the skin by killing bacteria responsible for inflamed acne.
